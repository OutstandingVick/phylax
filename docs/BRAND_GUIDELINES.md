# Phylax Brand Guidelines

Phylax means guardian or sentinel. The mark is a geometric shield-gate that subtly forms a `P` through the inner negative space. It is designed to work at 16px, in monochrome, and on light or dark institutional fintech surfaces.

## Logo

- Primary mark: `/public/brand/phylax-mark.svg`
- App icon: `/public/brand/phylax-app-icon.svg`
- Horizontal wordmark: `/public/brand/phylax-wordmark.svg`
- Light and dark lockups: `/public/brand/phylax-logo-light.svg`, `/public/brand/phylax-logo-dark.svg`

Minimum clear space is one half of the mark width on every side. Use the mark on solid cream, dark forest, or quiet surface tones. Do not stretch, rotate, add glow effects, place on busy imagery, or combine it with token logos.

## Color Tokens

Dark mode is the primary default.

- Background `#0B1110`
- Surface `#121A17`
- Surface soft `#1A241F`
- Primary `#7EBC89`
- Primary soft `#C1DBB3`
- Accent `#FE5D26`
- Warning `#F2C078`
- Text main `#FAEDCA`
- Text muted `#C1DBB3`

Light mode uses cream surfaces, dark forest text, green success states, orange risk states, and yellow warnings. Charts must combine color with labels or shapes.

## Typography

Use Sora or a close geometric sans for display and Inter/system sans for product UI. Data uses tabular numerals. Wordmark is uppercase `PHYLAX` with strong spacing.

## Components

Cards are calm, bordered, and functional. Controls use familiar icons, clear labels, keyboard focus, and visible states. Avoid meme aesthetics, floating coins, generic crypto gradients, and unverified compliance badges.

## Tone

Watchful, calm, precise, non-custodial, agent-native. Phylax provides risk analysis, not financial advice. Future capabilities must be labelled as future goals.

## Data Visualization

Use green for secure/approved/healthy, orange for urgent risk, yellow for warnings. Never use color alone. Always show score, label, confidence, and freshness when risk output is displayed.
